Talents in Spotlight
Srikanth Bolla
The first blind student to graduate from MIT, who went on to become the CEO and founder of a company with a turnover of 100 crores
Srikanth Bolla, the first blind student to graduate from MIT, is the CEO and Founder of Bollant Industries, which creates employment for specially-abled individuals. Despite being advised to abandon him at birth, Srikanth's parents supported him through numerous challenges. His tenacity led him to graduate from one of the world's most prestigious institutions and become a dynamic industrialist with a hundred-crore turnover.
Srikanth also founded a braille printing press at the Samanvai Center in Hyderabad. His journey of overcoming social prejudices and embracing self-belief is now immortalized in the biopic ‘Srikanth,’ starring Rajkummar Rao, released in May 2024.

Sumukhi Suresh
A multitalented powerhouse of the comedy world, she is an Improviser, Sketch Artist, Stand Up Comedian and Actor
Sumukhi Suresh, a renowned stand-up comedian, improv, and sketch artist, gained prominence with her viral videos and co-writing acclaimed sketch shows like 'Sketch in the City' and 'Go Straight Take Left’. She skyrocketed to fame with her web series 'Pushpavalli,' where she also won accolades as Best Actress at the Istanbul Film Festival.
Sumukhi's comedic talent extends across platforms, from Comedy Central's 'The Living Room' to YouTube's 'Better Life Foundation' and Amazon Prime's 'Comicstaan.' Notably, she founded 'Motormouth,' a company that focuses on film, show, and branded content creation. A trailblazer for women in comedy, Sumukhi combines sharp wit with a keen eye for the absurd, making her a standout in India's comedic landscape.

Closing Ceremony of Maharashtra Premier League in Pune’s MCA Stadium

Abhijeet Sawant

The closing ceremony of Maharashtra Premier League in MCA Stadium, Pune was nothing short of spectacular! Gracing the stage was the first Indian Idol winner, and music sensation, Abhijeet Sawant, who created an electrifying atmosphere with his performance. From his iconic chartbuster single ‘Mohabattein Lutaunga’ to yet another soothing single ‘Lafzo Mein Keh Na Sakoon’, he got the audience to take a trip down memory lane. But the entertainment did not stop there! Post the innings break, Abhijeet turned up the energy, thrilling the audience with hit songs like 'Zingaat' and 'Dolby Walyacha.'

Asian Paints Classic Privilege Club Meet in Barcelona

Welcome Hostesses

Quick Change Act

Crystal Balance Act

Asian Paints returned with yet another spectacular gathering for their Classic Privilege Club in Barcelona. It started with a warm welcome of the guests at the airport who were greeted by the graceful presence of Welcome Hostesses dressed in elegant attire. The welcome dinner on Day 1 was elevated to a whole new level of excitement with a mesmerizing Quick Change Act. This jaw-dropping performance left the audience spellbound as the artists effortlessly transformed their costumes in the blink of an eye. The gala dinner on Day 4 featured the enchanting Crystal Balance Act which was a delicate dance of equilibrium and grace, where the artist balanced shimmering crystals with extraordinary poise. The grand finale on Day 4 featured a dynamic Stunt Duo known for their incredible acrobatics performances, who wowed the audience with their strength and agility. Paired with the pulsating rhythms of the LED Drummers, whose illuminated instruments created a visual feast, the night was a perfect blend of sight and sound.

Herbalife’s 25 Years Celebrations in Delhi and Bangalore

Shaan

Vijay Prakash

As Herbalife India approached its 25th anniversary, the celebrations started with the spectacular Herbalife India Extravaganza.
